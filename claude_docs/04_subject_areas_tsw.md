Subject Areas define **what data the AI is allowed to see**.
The AI never sees the full TSW schema.

## MVP Subject Areas
1. Owners / Members
2. Contracts
3. Reservations
4. Inventory

Each Subject Area defines:
- Approved tables/views
- Approved columns
- Business-friendly labels
- Sensitivity classification
- Allowed roles

Subject Areas are configurable per tenant via the Admin UI.
