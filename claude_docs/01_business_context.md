# Business Context

## Audience
- Employees of Time Share companies
- Internal operational, finance, reservations, and management staff
- Not consumers and not public-facing

## Core Problems
- Dependence on IT for routine reports
- Inconsistent definitions of metrics across teams
- Manual spreadsheet and ad-hoc query workflows
- Risk of unsafe or non-compliant database access

## Business Goals
- Enable safe self-service analytics
- Centralize business logic via reusable rules
- Reduce reporting turnaround time
- Maintain strong governance, security, and auditability
